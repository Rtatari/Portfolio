about me

I love to code in html, css, javascript, because they would allowe me to bring my imaginations and ideas into beautiful, high resolution, screens in variaty of sizes.

Besides coding, I also enjoy working in groups and learn from individuals as they can suggest different views and bring different ideas into a project. More importantly what makes a good project is the process and the way that us designers deal with these problems. As a result, brainstorming, sketching, rendering, and iterating becomes very crucial aspects of a design project.

On the other hand, I also enjoy working with models and bringing the initial idea into a more livable, physical product. This could be either 3D modelling using programs like Solidworks, and Maya, or just physical models made with foam choards.
